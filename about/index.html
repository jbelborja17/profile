About Me

I am a Senior Software Engineer with professional experience since 2005, currently focused on adopting Artificial Intelligence across the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) to improve engineering productivity, software quality, and delivery effectiveness.

My background is centered on backend and platform engineering, with deep expertise in JVM ecosystems (Java, Kotlin) and solid experience in TypeScript. I design and evolve production-grade systems using clean and hexagonal architectures, applying Domain-Driven Design (DDD) principles in both monolithic and distributed (microservices) environments.

I have extensive hands-on experience with AWS, working across cloud-native services, Infrastructure as Code, and event-driven architectures. I have designed and operated workflow-based systems using Temporal and AWS Step Functions, always with a strong focus on reliability, scalability, and operability.

On the data side, I have worked with both relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL) and NoSQL solutions (DynamoDB), choosing persistence models based on domain and system requirements rather than trends.

I maintain a strong production and delivery mindset, with solid experience in automated testing, continuous delivery, and DevOps practices. I care deeply about building systems that are maintainable, observable, and aligned with real business needs.

Outside of day-to-day engineering, I enjoy evolving engineering practices, mentoring other engineers, and exploring how AI can be applied responsibly and effectively to modern software development.
